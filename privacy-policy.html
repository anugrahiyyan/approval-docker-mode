Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

1. Overview

This Leave Request Web Application is developed using Google Apps Script and integrated with Google Sheets. It operates entirely within your Google Workspace domain or your organization's approved environment.

2. Information We Collect

The application only stores information entered by users in the leave request form, including:

  • Full Name
  • Department
  • Email Address
  • Leave Dates
  • Leave Type & Reason

3. How We Use Your Information

The information is used solely for processing and managing leave requests within your organization. It is not shared with any third-party service outside of Google Workspace.

4. Data Security

All form data is stored securely in a Google Sheet accessible only to authorized users. Email addresses and leave details are used exclusively for internal communication and tracking purposes.

5. No Third-Party Access

This application does not integrate with or send data to any third-party service outside of Google’s own infrastructure.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact internal administrator or HR department.
